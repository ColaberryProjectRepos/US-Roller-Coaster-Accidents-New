Creating Calendar Table 10


1. On Power BI desktop under Modeling click on New table button 
2. Type the Dax formula below in the Dax formula bar, Click on the check mark, and save

Calander = ADDCOLUMNS(CALENDARAUTO(),"Year",YEAR([Date]),"YearMonth", FORMAT([Date],"YYYY-MM"),"Month Number",MONTH([Date]), "Month Name short",FORMAT([Date],"MMM"))
